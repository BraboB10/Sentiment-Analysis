Polarity refers to the emotional tone or sentiment expressed in a piece of text. Polarity scores are on a range from -1 to 1 where, -1 is a highly negative statement, 0 is a neutral statement, and 1 is a highly positive statment. Sentences like "I love you" tend to have a positive polarity and sentences like "I hate you" tend to have a negative polarity.

Subjectivity refers to how opinionated or objective the text is. Subjectivity is on a range of 0 to 1 where 0 means a statment is highly factual and 1 which means that a statement is highly opinionated. Sentences like "The sky is blue" tend to have a score closer to 0 and sentences like "This is the worst movie ever" tends to have a score closer to 1 as it is highly opinionated.
